Project Testing

Measuring success is important regardless of the industry. Smarter Gifter is no different. In order to effectively measure the success of Smarter Gifter, Phronesis Collective will do so through Key Performance Indicators (KPIs). This will ensure that we have a measurable, trustworthy base where we can further develop the project. Getting a clear and accurate picture of exactly how our users use Smarter Gifter will be crucial in delivering a product they want in a commercially viable way.

The specific KPIs that Phronesis Collective will utilise:

  • retention rate – percentage of customers we retain
  • churn rate - the percentage of customers that are actively choosing to keep the app
  • daily active users – percentage of users per day
  • monthly active users – percentage of users per month
  • cost per acquisition – average cost per purchase
  • average revenue per user.

Customer satisfaction is also important. At the end of the day, it is what will drive the business model. In order to determine customer satisfaction, Phronesis Collective will monitor:

  • Customer feedback
  • Subscription rates
  • Upgrades
  • Growth rate
  • User engagement
  • Reviews
  • Ratings

Phronesis Collective will conclude that Smarter Gifter has been a successful business venture once we have achieved our return on investment, as outlined in our Financial Analysis, Chapter 6 of this report.
