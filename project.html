Smarter Gifter - Description

Topic

Smarter Gifter mobile app will revolutionise the gift buying experience by providing calendar notifications and recommended gift suggestions to its users.

Using Smarter Gifter will be accessible, interactive, and intuitive. Once the user has downloaded the app to their preferred smart device, they can create individual profiles of their nearest and dearest. Profile creation is easy, and once created, will only require minimal maintenance.

As a trigger date approaches, the user will receive an automated notification sent directly to them via push notification or email, reminding them of the approaching celebrations.

Because Smarter Gifter is built around in-app profiles, it will make targeted recommendations and suggestions of gifts based on interests, age, hobbies, and location.

Smarter Gifter will provide the user the opportunity to shop directly online directly through store weblinks.

Motivation

At Phronesis Collective, we have created an app that will streamline the tracking of occasions and the purchasing of gifts for loved ones. Those who find gift purchasing a daunting task will readily welcome Smarter Gifter.

Conventional advertising can render buyers overwhelmed by choice. This often leads to distraction, loss of focus, and a risk of the overspending, impulse buying or last-minute shopping.

‘38% of US consumers say that guessing what people want is their greatest annoyance when shopping for presents’ (Emarsys 2021). Such is the issue with the gift giving roulette, that Australian retailer Myer created an entire 2021 advertisement campaign surrounding it. Titled Unriddle Christmas, Myer (2021) explores the dreaded anxiety around ensuring you give the perfect gift to the ones you love.

This gift-giving uncertainty is supported by Phronesis Collective market research.
A survey of 50 people told us that deciding on the perfect gift and the chaos of the shops were their biggest stressors at Christmas time.

Landscape

There are many family-orientated calendars and gifting apps on the market that aim to keep loved ones organised and connected. Most of these applications host collaboration features, calendars, lists, and schedules that the user can update and personalise. Some of our potential competitors include:

Christmas Gift List - An app specifically for Christmas gift purchases. The app allows users to track gift ideas by entering them in the app. Users can set a budget for each user and mark the gift off their list once the user has purchased it. On 01 January every year, lists will be archived and a new list with existing people will be generated automatically (engApps 2022).

Family Shared Calendar - A shared family schedule that allows users to share calendars, lists, notes, and assign tasks through Android and IOS devices. Information is shared through the app using one account with multiple users. Users utilise their own email address and a shared password (Beesoft Apps 2022).

GiftPlanner - A gift list and organiser that allows the user to keep track of gift list and events. Users can update their planned gift information, preferred stores, purchase statuses (including the order and tracking details). The app also allows users to browse the internet and save links directly to their app lists without the need to close the internet and open the app (Milham 2021).

We analysed the e-commence and m-commence markets and witnessed algorithm-based push-advertising by sellers. While some users enjoy watching pop-up advertisements and emails related to recent search of their interests, many others feel overwhelmed and information overloaded, if not annoyed, by such “predictive” marketing.

Our Smarter Gifter is designed to let our users to take control of what product information they like to receive, therefore, our Smarter Gifter is a buyer’s platform.

What sets Smarter Gifter apart from our competitors is our gift-giving calendar for all occasion, that send automated and solicited reminders to the User when they want it. It also has integrated AI that will provide the User with some suggestions selected specifically for the person the User is purchasing for.
